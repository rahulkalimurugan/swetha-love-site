Hey My Queen… ❤️

I don’t think you even realize how much you’ve changed my life.

But nothing ever feels enough.

I love you more than I know how to explain.

like it was nothing.

That’s when I knew… you’re different.

We didn’t do anything crazy… just walked…
and then that hug…

I don’t think you know how much that meant to me too.

That day we bought rings…

Like we weren’t just two people hanging out anymore…

Something that lasts.

All our hospital dates…
a little stressful… a little confusing…

but being there with you made everything feel okay.

Every new experience with you just… means more.

Timezone… bowling… claw machines…
us just being stupid and laughing non-stop…

I won’t lie… I was impressed.

The day you met my parents…
and we went to see that house…

Like I was actually looking at our future… with you in it.

But I promise you…

Always ❤️

No matter what happens…
no matter where life takes us…

Again… and again… and again.
